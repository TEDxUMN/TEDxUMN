The Coming Wave

Saturday May 11th | 5:30 PM - 10:00 PM | McNamera Alumni Center


We are surrounded by thousands of cultures, emerging technologies, and ideas. The next generation of thinking, acting, and learning is upon us. TEDxUMN's 2019 conference brings The Coming Wave of innovation and cultural change to the forefront of discourse with the aim of bringing the diverse Twin Cities community together. Regardless of our color, creed, religion, race, or orientation, The Coming Wave will wash over all of us.
